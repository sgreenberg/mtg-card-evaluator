How to Play

Welcome to the Perplexing Test!

1. A Magic: The Gathering card will be displayed.

2. Evaluate its strength for limited play (draft/sealed).

3. Select a grade (F to A+) using the buttons below the card.

4. Your guess will be compared against an expert rating.

5. Review the card's details, win rate, and commentary.

Click anywhere on the screen to advance to the next card after seeing the results.

Use the menu for options like starting a new game.

About

Perplexing Test - Tarkir Dragonstorm Edition

This application is designed to help Magic: The Gathering players practice and improve their card evaluation skills for limited formats (like Draft and Sealed).

Hone your "vibe check" instincts and get ready for your next event!

Card data and ratings are for a custom Tarkir Dragonstorm set.
